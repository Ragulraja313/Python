ABOUT US

Samsung smartwatches are portable devices worn on the wrist that apps and act as an extension of your mobile. They offer numerous functions that can have big impact on dayto-day life, including advanced health and fitness tracking, seamless integration with other Galaxy devices, convenient access to apps and services, long battery life, andized style options. Samsung Galaxy smartwatches operate using an eSIM that is automatically downloaded to the watch during the initial pairing process. They can track various activities using built-in GPS and Samsung Health, and send both text and voice messages. Smartwatches can also display notifications and make contactless payments using Samsung Pay.

Samsung is a South Korean company that special in the production of a wide variety of consumer industry electronics, appliances, digital devices, semiconductors, memory chips, and integrated systems. The company was founded in 1938 and has since become one of the world's largest producers of electronic devices, with a significant presence in the global market. Samsung's product offerings include mobile devices, TVs, home appliances, computing products, displays, and accessories, among others. The company is committed to complying with local laws and regulations, as well as applying a strict global code of conduct to all employees, with the goal of becoming one of the most ethical companies in the world. Samsung's business philosophy is centered on creating superior products and services that contribute to a better global society, with a focus on human resource development and technical superiority.
